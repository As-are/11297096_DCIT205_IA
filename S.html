Research at Department of Computer Science

Digital Arts

At University of Ghana, researchers across many disciplines think critically about a rapidly-changing world. Multiple nodes of digital culture reflect and connect to mobilize engagement and innovation across culture, economy and society. Transdisciplinary research and research-creation connects artists, designers and engineers, and nurtures new ideas and challenges. Areas of investigations include:

  • Digital games as exemplary objects for creation, innovation and mediation
  • Bio art and artistic laboratory practices
  • Boundary-breaking performing arts
  • Maker culture and community engagement
